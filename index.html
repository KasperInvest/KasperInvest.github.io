About me

Analyst with a passion for valuation

I'm a Norwegian finance student and independent equity analyst with a strong interest in fundamental analysis, valuation, and capital markets. This site is my public work window — a place where I publish original research, market notes, and investment perspectives.

My goal is a career in equity research or investment management. I believe the best way to demonstrate capability is to actually do the work and publish it publicly — not just list it on a CV.

I cover primarily Nordic and European listed companies, with particular interest in technology, iGaming, pharma, and the intersection of macroeconomics and equity valuation.
